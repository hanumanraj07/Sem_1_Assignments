Dual-Chain Architecture

Consensus Mechanism

BNB Chain uses a Proof of Staked Authority (PoSA) consensus algorithm that combines the benefits of Proof of Stake (PoS) and Proof of Authority (PoA).

Key Features:

  • 21 Validators: A limited set of validators ensures high performance and fast finality
  • Staking: BNB holders can stake their tokens to participate in governance
  • Cross-chain Transfer: Seamless asset transfer between BNB Beacon Chain and BNB Smart Chain
  • Low Energy Consumption: Environmentally friendly compared to Proof of Work systems
